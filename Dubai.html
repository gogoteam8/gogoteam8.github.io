About Dubai



  • The picturesque city "Dubai" offers many wonderful hotels located on the eastern coast of the Arabian Peninsula, it is the second largest emirate after the Emirate of Abu Dhabi. Dubai hotels are exceptional because they are near various touristic attractions like Burj Khalifa; the tallest tower in the world and it is situated in downtown Dubai. From the top of the tower, you can view the landmarks of Dubai also many concerts and fireworks are held in this tower. Burj Khalifa involves hotel, offices and apartments with luxurious facilities. Jumeirah district in dubai is distinguished by the modest town houses, and surrounded by cafes, restaurants and shopping centers. Also, Jumeirah Beach is one of the most famous and charming beaches in Dubai that includes many special resorts.

  • Dubai Marina, also known as "Marsa Dubai" or "New Dubai", features dazzling skyscrapers, apartment complexes, restaurants, shops and cafes. There are other tourist places for entertainment, that many tourists from all over the world come to visit it like the shopping centers especially in the Global Village, which is the most famous entertaining spot in the region. It provides the guests with a series of stunning events in an open area, as well as the unique taste of shopping and a variety of international cuisines. Dubai Miracle Garden is the most beautiful and largest garden in the world and has more than 45 million different natural flowers with different shapes and odors. It is divided into corridors and arranged in colors and homogeneity to burnish the viewers.

The Main Airport



  • Dubai International Airport (IATA code: DXB) is classified as one of the best ten airports in the world and it is growing so fast

Shopping in Dubai



  • For shopping lovers and those who are interested in fashion, Dubai certainly should be your first choice, as it includes the largest and best shopping centers in the world. When you go shopping in Dubai, you practice a favorite hobby.

Shopping Areas and Main markets



  • Dubai Mall: is the largest mall in the world and one of the most entertaining places in the region for its great capacity. It is one of the best places for shopping, entertainment and tourism where you will find a lot of entertainment stuff popularized worldwide like the Dubai Aquarium.
  • Mall of the Emirates: is a large shopping center in Al Barsha, which contains many halls, cinemas, yard games, various shops and a large theater. The most distinguished thing is the indoor ski slope; it is the first ski slope in the Middle East.
  • Deira Mall: is one of the most famous shopping malls in Dubai. It has a shopping and entertainment center consists of two floors and includes more than 300 shops.

  • Burjuman Shopping Center: is characterized by satisfying the tourists' needs, where you will find many shopping shops for global and local brands. Various local traditional and international restaurants are available as well. Also entertainments places and play area for children.

Shopping Centers



  • The "Gold Souk" also known as "Souk Al Dhab" involves more than 300 shops for gold selling, for this, it is one of the largest gold markets in the world.
  • "Spice Souk" is one of the oldest retail markets in the old Dubai; it is located in Deira next to the Gold Souk. It includes many of the traditional narrow lanes filled with shops, where the visitors can find what they crave from rare to well-known spices.
