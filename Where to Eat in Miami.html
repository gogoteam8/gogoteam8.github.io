Where to Eat in Miami


Miami, FL •Updated May 19, 2020


Perhaps, great food might not be the very first thing that comes to mind when you think about Miami: South Beach and the 1972 Dolphins are worthy contenders.
                       Now, let me convince you otherwise.


The All Time Favorites


Long layover in Miami and you only have time for one meal? That's okay, just make sure it's one of these restaurants.



Palmar


Food & Drinks




Cuisine: Chinese

Price: $$

Always Order: Softshell Crab Fried Rice & Roasted Duck

Palmar was named by Bon Appetit magazine one of America's 50 best new restaurants in
                    2018 (in ALL of the United States of America, not just in the Mall
                    of the Americas). The combo of spicy, crunchy and vibrant Chinese food with an ice cold
                    beer might be the
                   best you can get in a hot Miami summer night.







Alter


Food & Drinks


















Cuisine: American/Experimental

Price: $$

Always Order: Tasting Menu

The accolades for Alter and chef Brad Kilgore keep piling up: Multiple James Beard award nominations, one of the '21 best new restaurants in America' by Eater and 'Best New Chef in the country' according to Food & Wine. Alter's modern and unique menu, yet incredibely delicous, makes this place a one of a kind dining experience in Miami.
