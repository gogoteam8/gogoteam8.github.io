About Riyadh



  • The capital of Saudi Arabia, "Riyadh" is popular for the wonderful hotels, it is considered as the largest and the most famous city in Saudi Arabia. Riyadh hotels are concentrated in main three locations. The first one is the North of Riyadh, where you will find a lot of Riyadh hotels near the famous touristic attractions such as the Al Faisaliah Tower which is designed hierarchically and ends with an elegant golden ball. It also includes the commercial market and various business offices as well as a large number of luxurious hotels. The second location is down town, which is considered as one of the most vital areas in Riyadh and includes many important monuments. Such as, The Kingdom Centre; it is one of the biggest shopping Centers in Riyadh. The National Museum highlights the bright history of the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia. The historical Fort and Museum Masmak is part of the old town of Riyadh, and other archaeological and tourist places that many guests come from all over the world to visit. The third site is south Riyadh city, where you can easily reach the hotels of Riyadh through King Khalid International Airport, which is about 20 km from the center of Riyadh. Also you can visit the zoo, which is one of the best entertainment places for both adults and children.

The Main Airport


  • King Khalid Airport (IATA code: RUH) consists of 4 passenger terminals in addition to the Royal Hall.

Shopping in Riyadh


  • Shopping in the city of Riyadh has a private and pleasure taste. At first you may find that the prices are high, but through negotiations you can get suitable prices.

Shopping Areas and Main markets


  • Kingdom Centre Tower: is one of the most prestigious and luxurious shopping centers in Riyadh, its height is 302.3 m. It includes celebrations hall, libraries, offices, luxury apartments, and hotels. Also a large number of international restaurants, in addition to King Abdullah Mosque which is the second highest mosque in World.

  • Al Faisaliah Tower: is one of the most prominent and famous places in Riyadh at Olaya district and its height reach 266 meters. It consists of shopping centers with major world brands, several luxurious hotels and other buildings

  • Hayat Mall: is located in King Abdul Aziz Street and contains everything you need from the shops that are filled with world-famous brands, as well as gift shops, and there are many restaurants and cafes to enjoy shopping.

Shopping Centers


  • Shopping centers represent a large part of Riyadh, and the most important malls are "Carrefour" in Al khaimah markets, which is characterized by the diversity of products like fast foods, fish, clothes and household items which are available at the lowest prices.
  • The "Khurais Mall" is famous for the outfits for all people from different nationalities, it is located in the Khurais Road, and there is also a park for children for more recreation and entertainment.

  • "Granada Mall" is one of the best malls in Riyadh at the Imam Abdullah bin Saud bin Abdul Aziz Street. It contains more than 30 restaurants and cafe. For entrance and exit, there are 16 gates and this will facilitate reaching all the directions in the center, and also includes a number of global and local and popular shops.
